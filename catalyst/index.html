The Catalyst Summit

It is a yearly career fair with over 3,000 in-house and online attendance with cardinal aim of equipping and strategizing on building amazing career portfolio. Our career fair is the best in the nation and we have helped more people get hired through our events than any other company out there. If you are looking for a job or a career, you have come to the right place.

LEARN

A relaxed, respectful atmosphere where attendances can ask questions to various career opportunities

DIVERSE SPEAKERS

We'll have speakers with experience from all around the world, including Africa, Europe, and America give talks on different career opportunities both onsites and remotely

LOCATION

This year's event will be holding in Multi-Purpose Hall, University of Lagos
